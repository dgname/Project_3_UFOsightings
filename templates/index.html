UFO Sightings in Mexico

Welcome to the UFO Sightings Dashboard. This project visualizes reported UFO sightings in Mexico, allowing you to explore the data by UFO shape, city, and more.

Use the interactive map to see UFO sightings categorized by shape or explore the cities with the highest number of sightings.

Animated UFO Sightings Map

This animated map visualizes the progression of UFO sightings in Mexico over the years. Each sighting is represented by a marker, and the map cycles through the years to show how reports have changed over time.
